Squid Game

Preface

Netflix series "Squid Game" has become a global sensation recently.
Our studio recreates six Korean childhood games of "Squid Game" in Minecraft.
Including the hall of beds, "Red Light, Green Light," "Dalgona Candy," "Tug of War," "Marble," "Bridge Cross," and "Squid Game."
Seven classical scenes, six delicate games, and an unlimited number of players. Is this hell? Or the world of rules?

Game Scenes

Hall of Beds

The hall is full of beds on high shelves, and the scoreboard shows that more than half of the players agreed to continue.
Who will survive this game?

First Game: Red Light, Green Light

Remember to stop your steps when the time is up, or you will be caught.

Second Game: Dalgona Candy

As a player, which one of the four shapes should you choose?
Finally, every player should poke Dalgona candy in Minecraft.

Third Game: Tug of War

No matter who you are, you can play such a fair game.
Choose the team here. Will you survive or change the rules to lose the game?

Forth Game: Marble

Standing in the corner of the street at sunset, you have to guess the quantities of marbles.
Is it odd? Or is it even? Get 20 marbles to win. Undoubtedly, there will be no small stones on the floor to pick up!

Fifth Game: Bridge Cross

As a player, the only chance to survive is moving forward.
Is there anyone who has insight into the game?

Sixth Game: Squid Game

Let's have a real Squid Game in the final!
The Squid Game is just like the Tag. Experience the most exciting attacking and defending side battle in the game.
