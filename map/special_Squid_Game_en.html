Squid Game

Preface

Netflix series "Squid Game" has become a global sensation recently.
Our studio recreates six Korean childhood games of "Squid Game" in Minecraft.
Including the hall of beds, "Red Light, Green Light," "Dalgona Candy," "Tug of War," "Marbles," "Bridge Cross," and "Squid Game."
Seven classical scenes, six delicate games, and an unlimited number of players. Is this hell? Or the world of rules?
